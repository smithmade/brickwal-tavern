NEIL WEST
EXECUTIVE CHEF

A man of few words and many accolades,
Chef West has been at the forefront of
the New Jersey dining scene for the past
25 years. Before joining Smith as the
executive chef of Brickwall, he was
executive chef for the Grenville Hotel and
Bretton Woods Inn and a chef at David
Burke’s Fromagerie, where he was chosen
to create a six-course menu for the
Confrerie de la Chaine des Rotisseurs, the
oldest and largest food and wine society.
Yet it is at Brickwall that his talent for high-
end comfort food truly thrives. As part of
Smith’s executive culinary team, Chef West
is a key voice in the creation of dishes for
Smith’s upcoming restaurants.
